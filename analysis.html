The Profitability Gap

Detailed breakdown of Ather's revenue-to-profit journey reveals structural inefficiencies that prevent sustainable profitability

Revenue to Profit Waterfall

Key Insight: Cost Structure

82% of revenue is consumed by COGS, leaving only 18% gross margin to cover operating expenses. This is significantly higher than the 70-75% industry benchmark.

Source: Ather FY26 Financial Statements

Competitive Context

TVS achieves 25-28% gross margins through superior supply chain integration and scale economies, enabling profitable operations despite lower ASP.

Source: Industry Analysis & Benchmarking

Critical Finding

R&D and Sales & Marketing expenses alone (₹353 Cr) consume the entire gross profit, leaving no buffer for operational improvements or market expansion.
